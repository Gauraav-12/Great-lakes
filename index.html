Exploring the Great Lakes

Overview

The Great Lakes are a group of five large freshwater lakes in North America. They are Lake Superior, Lake Michigan, Lake Huron, Lake Erie, and Lake Ontario.

Highlights of Each Lake

  • Lake Superior

    Lake Superior is the largest and deepest of the Great Lakes.

  • Lake Michigan

    Lake Michigan is the only Great Lake that is entirely within the United States.

  • Lake Huron

    Lake Huron has the longest shoreline of the Great Lakes.
